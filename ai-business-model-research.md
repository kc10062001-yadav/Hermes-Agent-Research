# PART 1: AI PRODUCTIZED SERVICES

## 1.1 What Are They?

**AI Productized Services** are standardized, repeatable AI-powered offerings sold at fixed prices — the opposite of custom consulting. You build the AI solution once and sell it many times.

**Key characteristics:**
- Delivered by AI, not human labor (or minimal human oversight)
- Fixed scope, fixed price (no custom work per client)
- Scalable — one AI system serves unlimited customers
- Digital delivery (Gumroad, Stan Store, LemonSqueezy, Apify Store, Zapier)
- Zero marginal cost after initial build

## 1.3 Pricing Models

1. **Flat-rate per deliverable**: $15-$297 (Fiverr model)
2. **Tiered bundles**: Basic ($27) / Premium ($97) with more features
3. **Pay-per-use/unit**: $0.02-$0.05 per API call (Apify model)
4. **Subscription/rental**: $19-$47/mo (less common in productized services — more in SaaS)
5. **One-time download**: $9-$97 (Gumroad digital goods model)

**Most common for beginners:** Flat-rate one-time or tiered ($27-$47 sweet spot for Gumroad, $15-$40 for Fiverr services).

# PART 2: AI RESELLING

## 2.1 What It Means

**AI Reselling** has three distinct models:

### Model A: Reselling API Access
- You buy AI API credits at wholesale and sell to end users at markup
- Examples: Reselling OpenAI API, Anthropic API, or aggregating multiple models
- **Margins: 10-30%** typically (thin)
- **Platforms:** OpenAI API (Terms of Service strictly prohibit sublicensing/resale), AWS Bedrock Marketplace
- **Reality check:** OpenAI ToS section 2(c) prohibits "sublicensing, reselling, or redistributing" API access. Most AI providers have similar restrictions.

### Model B: White-Label AI Tools
- You rebrand an existing AI SaaS under your own name and sell it
- Examples: White-label AI chatbots (**Lety.ai** found on GitHub), white-label GHL, white-label AI writing assistants
- **Margins: 30-80%**
- **Platforms found in research:**
  - **Lety.ai** — Complete white-label AI chatbot platform; resell under your own brand, no coding needed
  - **GoHighLevel Agency Pro** ($497/mo) — Resell CRM under your brand at $197/client netting ~$100 margin/client
  - BotPenguin, Tidio, ManyChat (white-label tiers available)
- **Margins:** Typically $50-$200/client/month profit depending on tier

### Model C: Reselling SaaS (Affiliate)
- Standard SaaS affiliate/reseller programs
- Example: GoHighLevel pays 40% recurring monthly for life ($118.80/mo per Unlimited customer)
- Example: Carrd pays 30% on Pro upgrades ($5.70 per upgrade)

**Key finding from "Computational Arbitrage in AI Model Markets" paper (2026):** Arbitrage strategies in AI model markets can generate significant profits by efficiently allocating inference budgets across providers, but margins compress over time as provider pricing converges.

## 2.4 Legal Considerations

### Critical: Most AI Companies Prohibit API Resale
OpenAI's Terms of Service explicitly state:
- Section 2(c): "You may not... sublicense, resell, or redistribute the Services"
- Section 3(a): API keys are "personal, non-transferable"

**Penalties:** Account termination, loss of all credits, potential legal action. This is NOT a viable business model for individuals.

### White-Label Reselling is Legal
- White-labeling SaaS is standard B2B practice
- The provider explicitly allows it (e.g., Lety.ai's whole business model is reselling)
- Must comply with: local business registration, GST/VAT, data privacy (India's DPDP Act 2023)

### Affiliate Marketing is Simplest
- No legal complexity
- No ToS concerns
- Just disclosure requirements

- **"AIAA $10k/month"**: Possible but requires sales skills. Not realistic in 3-4 weeks.

### What's Underrated for India
- **GoHighLevel affiliate** (40% recurring = ₹10,216/mo per referral)
- **Fiverr** — India's cost advantage
- **Gumroad** — Accepts Indian creators, $0 subscription
- **White-label chatbots** — Huge demand from Indian small businesses
